# Privacy Policy — Hebrew RTL

**Last updated:** March 23, 2026

## Summary

Hebrew RTL does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

## Data Collection

This extension collects **no user data** of any kind. Specifically:

- No personal information is collected
- No browsing history is accessed or stored
- No page content is read, recorded, or transmitted
- No analytics or usage tracking is performed
- No crash reports are sent to any server
- No external network requests are made by this extension

## Data Storage

The only data stored by this extension is your **configuration** (CSS selectors and enabled/disabled state per domain). This configuration is stored exclusively in Chrome's built-in `chrome.storage.sync`, which is managed entirely by your Google account. It never leaves your account and is not accessible to the extension developer.

## Third Parties

This extension does not share any data with third parties. No third-party scripts, SDKs, or services are included.
